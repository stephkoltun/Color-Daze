Color Daze reduces a single day to its color palette. Each image from the selected day is represented by its dominant color. When seen together, the color spectrum of the day is evident.

The site uses personal images from my travels and Brian Gonzalez' RGBaster javascript library.

This is a side project by Stephanie Koltun.
